Release Notes version 1 POC

-Adds the main sprites that will be the focus of the game
-Each asteroid has their own spawn point which randomly happens over a certain amount of time
-Adds the player controls to the "spirit" sprite; player controls the sprite by moving up and down
-When player collides with asteroid, the asteroid sprite is destroyed on impact
